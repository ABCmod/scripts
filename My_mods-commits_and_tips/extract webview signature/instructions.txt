extract the webview apk (e.g. Chrome stable apk) with a zip archiver
extract Keystore explorer zip
open the program with: java -jar kse.jar
click on Examine Certificate
choose the META-INF/CERT.RSA file from the extracted webview 
click on Examine
click on PEM and get the signature

Download latest official keystore explorer from: http://keystore-explorer.org/index.html
